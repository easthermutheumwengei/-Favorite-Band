BAND MEMBERS

MAUREEN KUNGA

Maureen Muthoni Kunga is a Kenyan musician and a lawyer. She is a female vocalist in band three-member band, Elani . Her Musical Journey, she says, started from her appearances at the church choir whereby she was able to nurture her talent to the person she is today. Kunga is a member of the popular Elani band. She is undeniably one of the most charming and talented singers. Her mellow voice with her striking looks makes her auspicious in the three-member band. Elani is a Kenyan African pop group from Nairobi. It consists of three members namely, Wambui Ngugi, Brian Chweya, and Maureen Kunga. The trio released their debut single”Jana Usiku” in November 2013 that became a great success, creating a pavement for their subsequent singles. In 2014, the trio released numerous singles in the making of their debut album Barua ya Dunia.

BRIAN CHWEYA

Brian chweya is the well known as the only man in the Elani music Band. He is a kenyan musician,singer,songwriter,guitarist and alsoa lawyer. Born in Nairobi as the first child in his family,he attended Moi Educational center for his primary education and Upper Hill school,KCPE.Later attended University of Nairobi where he earned his degree in Law and still went ahead to obtain his postgraduate Diploma in Law from Kenya school of Law.

WAMBUI NGUGI

Wambui Ngugi graduated with Bsc.in Acturial Science from the University of Nairobi in 2012.Wambuis voice is a hard voice to miss.She is a member of Elani band group that amazes the country with great relatable music,song after song. In 2008, together with brian chweya,and maureen Kunga, they formed the band Elani.
