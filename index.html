ELANI BAND

Elani is a well known hiphop kenyan group.It comprises of three members namely; Brian chweya,Maureen kunga and Wambui Ngugi

The three members released their debut sinle "jana usiku" in november 2013 that became a great success,creating a pavement on thier subsequent singles. In the year 2014,they released numerous single in the making of their debut album barua ya Dunia.
